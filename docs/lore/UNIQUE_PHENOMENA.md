### Prism Chorus Faultline (`phenomenon.prism-chorus-fault`)
- **Location & Anchors:** Catalyzed near `anchor.prism-spire.auric-step` when Glassfall showers graze the ridge network feeding `settlement.glasswake-relay`.
- **Resonance Impact:** Dissonant Prismwell chords throw `tech.tier.switchline-flux` talons into oscillation, forcing emergency Cooling Interludes and joint diagnostics between the Prismwell Kite Guild (`faction.prismwell-kite-guild`) and Tempered Accord auditors (`faction.tempered-accord`).
- **Narrative Usage:** Ideal for crisis-to-diplomacy arcs where sky caravans negotiate corridor reprioritization; presents opportunities for players to propose creative stabilization rituals that still respect resonance band limits.
- **Story Consolidation Tags:** `sc.phenomenon.prism-chorus-fault`, `ner.phenomenon.prism-chorus-fault`, `sc.tech.tension.switchline`, `ner.tech.tier.switchline`.
- **Moderation Triggers:** `mod.review.phenomenon.prism-chorus-fault` mirrors DES-MOD-01 rulebooks; tie flagged transcripts back to `mod.review.tech.switchline-flux` to audit attempted spectrum breaches.
- **Status:** Canon — referenced in `COSMOLOGY.md` Umbra Wake countermeasures and Switchline quota tensions in `TECHNOLOGY.md`.
